Practical and Dynamic Methods

Our monitoring is fully specialized and personalized. We offer classes for both independent and occasional periods of doubt solving issues, or weekly planning for school in which the teacher focuses on the dynamics of the student's school. We try to follow the most the curriculum of regular school classes, following their materials, methodologies.

The monitoring aims to teach school students to create a routine of studies, so that in time they will be able to study alone in regular way, and specially create a routine of organized and enjoyable studies.
